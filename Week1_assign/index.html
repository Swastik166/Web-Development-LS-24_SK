Game Overview

Formula 1 is the pinnacle of motorsport, featuring the world's fastest cars and most skilled drivers. Originating in 1950, F1 has become a global phenomenon, combining cutting-edge technology with high-speed racing.


Each F1 season consists of a series of races, known as Grands Prix, held worldwide. Teams compete for two titles: the Drivers' Championship and the Constructors' Championship.


Current Drivers Champion is Max Verstappen and Constructors Champion is Red Bull Racing who dominated the 2023 season with 21/22 race wins going to red bull out of which 19 were of Max
